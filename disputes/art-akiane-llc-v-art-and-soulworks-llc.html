Art Akiane LLC v. Art & SoulWorks LLC

U.S. District Court, N.D. Ill. · US (IL) · 2020 · Decided

Citation/Docket: 2020 WL 5593242 (N.D. Ill. Sept. 18, 2020)

Parties & Funders

Posture: Discovery dispute
Funder(s) involved: (Undisclosed)

Background

Defendant sought funding-related discovery in an IP dispute.

Holding & Outcome

Court found funding discovery irrelevant and potentially harmful to plaintiff; denied.

Practical Lesson

Relevance, not privilege, is often the cleanest shield for funding materials.
