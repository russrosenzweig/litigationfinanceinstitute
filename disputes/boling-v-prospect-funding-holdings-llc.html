Champerty & maintenance

Boling v. Prospect Funding Holdings, LLC

U.S. Court of Appeals, Sixth Circuit (Kentucky law) · US (KY) · 2018 · Decided

Citation/Docket: 771 F. App'x 562 / No. 17-6383 (6th Cir. 2018)

Parties & Funders

Posture: Funder vs. funded consumer plaintiff
Funder(s) involved: Prospect Funding Holdings

Background

Prospect sued to enforce pre-settlement advances against a Kentucky PI plaintiff.

Holding & Outcome

Applying Kentucky law, the court held the funding agreements champertous and void as against public policy.

Practical Lesson

State-by-state champerty mapping is existential for consumer funders; identical contracts are enforceable in one state and void next door.
