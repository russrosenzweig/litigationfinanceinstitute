Arbitration funding

Essar Oilfields Services Ltd. v. Norscot Rig Mgmt.

England & Wales High Court (arbitration appeal) · UK · 2016 · Decided

Citation/Docket: [2016] EWHC 2361 (Comm)

Parties & Funders

Posture: Recoverability of funding costs in arbitration
Funder(s) involved: Woodsford (funder of Norscot)

Background

An ICC tribunal awarded the winning party the cost of its litigation funding (including the funder's uplift) as 'other costs'; the loser challenged the award.

Holding & Outcome

Upheld — arbitrators may award third-party funding costs under the Arbitration Act.

Practical Lesson

In arbitration, funding costs can shift to the loser — a major economic asymmetry vs. court litigation that parties should price into strategy.
