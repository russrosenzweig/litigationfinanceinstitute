Fulton v. Foley

U.S. District Court, N.D. Ill. · US (IL) · 2019 · Decided

Citation/Docket: 2019 WL 6609298 (N.D. Ill. Dec. 5, 2019)

Parties & Funders

Posture: Discovery dispute
Funder(s) involved: (Consumer/civil-rights funding)

Background

Defendants sought litigation funding information from a funded plaintiff.

Holding & Outcome

Funding discovery denied as irrelevant absent a particularized showing.

Practical Lesson

The majority U.S. rule: no funding discovery without case-specific relevance — but the exception now has teeth (see Lituma).
