Consumer litigation funding

Oasis Legal Finance Group, LLC v. Coffman

Colorado Supreme Court · US (CO) · 2015 · Decided

Citation/Docket: 361 P.3d 400 (Colo. 2015)

Parties & Funders

Posture: Funders vs. state regulator (declaratory)
Funder(s) involved: Oasis Legal Finance; LawCash

Background

Funders sought a ruling that pre-settlement advances were not 'loans' under Colorado's consumer credit code.

Holding & Outcome

Held: the transactions are loans subject to the UCCC, despite contingent repayment.

Practical Lesson

The loan-vs-asset-purchase characterization battle is the core regulatory risk in consumer funding, and states split on it (contrast Ruth v. Cherokee).
