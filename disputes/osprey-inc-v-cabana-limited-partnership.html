Champerty & maintenance

Osprey, Inc. v. Cabana Limited Partnership

South Carolina Supreme Court · US (SC) · 2000 · Decided

Citation/Docket: 532 S.E.2d 269 (S.C. 2000)

Parties & Funders

Posture: Champerty defense to assigned claim
Funder(s) involved: Osprey (assignee/funder)

Background

Assignment of an interest in a real-estate lawsuit in exchange for funding was attacked as champertous.

Holding & Outcome

South Carolina abolished champerty as a defense, holding other doctrines police litigation abuse.

Practical Lesson

One of the watershed pro-funding decisions; frequently cited by courts retiring champerty.
