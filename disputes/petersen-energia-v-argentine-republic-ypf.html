Cross-border enforcement

Petersen Energia v. Argentine Republic (YPF)

U.S. District Court, S.D.N.Y.; enforcement worldwide · US/Intl · 2023- · Enforcement ongoing

Citation/Docket: No. 15-cv-2739 (S.D.N.Y.) ($16.1B judgment Sept. 2023)

Parties & Funders

Posture: Funded shareholders vs. sovereign
Funder(s) involved: Burford Capital (acquired claims out of Spanish insolvency)

Background

Burford acquired Petersen's claims through Spanish insolvency proceedings and funded the decade-long YPF expropriation case; Argentina has attacked Burford's role and returns throughout enforcement.

Holding & Outcome

$16.1B judgment for plaintiffs — the largest funder-driven outcome to date; Burford's share (a large majority of proceeds) is itself a public flashpoint; collection remains contested across jurisdictions.

Practical Lesson

Insolvency estates are a major funder acquisition channel, and mega-returns invite sovereign-level resistance and political scrutiny of the industry.
