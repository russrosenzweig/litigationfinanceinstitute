Champerty & maintenance

Wilson v. Harris

Alabama Court of Civil Appeals · US (AL) · 1996 · Decided

Citation/Docket: 688 So. 2d 265 (Ala. Civ. App. 1996)

Parties & Funders

Posture: Enforceability of funding agreement
Funder(s) involved: (Individual funder)

Background

Funding agreement for a share of lawsuit proceeds challenged under Alabama public policy.

Holding & Outcome

Void — a 'gambling contract' akin to champerty.

Practical Lesson

Alabama remains one of the most hostile jurisdictions; some contracts are unsalvageable by drafting.
