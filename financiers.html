Meet the Financiers

38 litigation funders, in one place.

Compiled against the Association of Litigation Funders (England & Wales), the International Legal Finance Association's member list, Chambers & Partners rankings, the Lawdragon 100 Global Leaders in Litigation Finance, Westfleet Advisors' industry Buyer's Guide, and the third-party-funding.org global directory. Profiles built strictly from what each firm and independent reporting have made public. Not an endorsement, and not the curated matching of the Exchange below — just the field, laid out plainly.

This is a large, real market — likely 60–100+ active funders worldwide once every small and regional shop is counted — and firms consolidate or restructure faster than any directory can track (see the Fortress–Therium entry in the Research Library for a recent example). Some entries above are thin because independent public reporting on that firm is thin; that's disclosed rather than papered over. Corrections welcome.
