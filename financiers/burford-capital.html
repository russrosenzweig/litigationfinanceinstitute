Litigation Funder Profile

Burford Capital

Public (NYSE/LSE: BUR) · Founded 2009 · New York

The largest publicly traded litigation finance company. Founded by Christopher Bogart and Jonathan Molot; ~$1.79B market cap and ~$399M trailing revenue as of early 2026. Two segments: Principal Finance and Asset Management.

Investment criteria: No fixed minimum publicly stated, but prefers large commercial matters with damages several multiples of budget. Contract, fraud, fiduciary duty, securities, antitrust, IP, insolvency, and international arbitration.
