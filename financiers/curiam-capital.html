Litigation Funder Profile

Curiam Capital

New York

Private investment firm focused on litigation financing for businesses and law firms; majority externally funded by investor Michael Platt.

Investment criteria: $1M+ for single cases, $3M+ for portfolios (3+ cases required). Strong in patent and commercial IP; explicitly excludes matrimonial, medical malpractice, and individual personal injury claims.
