Fortress Investment Group (Legal Finance)

~$70B AUM alternative manager · Legal finance arm since 2013

Deployed over $4B into litigation finance since 2013; roughly $6.8B in legal-asset exposure. In June 2025 assumed operational control of Therium Capital's global litigation portfolio (40+ active matters) following Therium's restructuring.
