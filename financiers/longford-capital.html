Litigation Funder Profile

Longford Capital

Illinois

Well-capitalized commercial funder especially known for intellectual property matters, alongside general commercial and antitrust disputes. ILFA founding member.

Investment criteria: $25M to over $1B in controversy. Business-to-business contract, antitrust, IP (patent, trademark, copyright, trade secret — a notable strength), fiduciary duty, fraud, bankruptcy, and arbitration claims.
