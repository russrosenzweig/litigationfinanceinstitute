Litigation Funder Profile

Nivalion

Zug/Steinhausen, Switzerland

European commercial litigation funder; principal Dr. Isabelle Berger named among the 2025 Lawdragon 100 Global Leaders in Litigation Finance.

Investment criteria: Usual minimum around €7M in dispute, though smaller claims are considered selectively. Cross-border disputes, international arbitration, and complex commercial litigation across Europe, the Americas, and Asia-Pacific.
