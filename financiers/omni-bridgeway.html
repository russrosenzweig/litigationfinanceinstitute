Litigation Funder Profile

Omni Bridgeway

Public (ASX) · Roots to 1986 · Global

Formed from IMF Bentham's 2019 acquisition of Omni Bridgeway; traces origins to Netherlands-based funding activity beginning in 1986. Widely described as the largest and most experienced dispute funding and enforcement/recovery specialist globally; Chambers Band 1 for Litigation Funding and Global Asset Tracing, 2026.
