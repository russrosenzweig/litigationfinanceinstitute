Litigation Funder Profile

Pravati Capital

Scottsdale, Arizona

Provides non-recourse capital advances, with a focus on mature personal injury and mass tort cases with an established settlement precedent, alongside law firm portfolio financing.

Investment criteria: One of the few institutional funders active in personal injury and mass tort, provided the case is mature with an established settlement precedent; also law firm portfolio financing.
