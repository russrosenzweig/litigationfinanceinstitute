Litigation Funder Profile

Therium Capital Management

UK · Founded 2009 — portfolio absorbed 2025

One of the UK's founding litigation funders and an original Association of Litigation Funders member. In June 2025, its global litigation portfolio was taken over by Fortress Investment Group following internal restructuring — treat current standalone status with caution.
