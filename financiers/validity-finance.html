Litigation Funder Profile

Validity Finance

New York

U.S. commercial litigation funder and ILFA founding member; active across general commercial disputes.

Investment criteria: Roughly $2-4M per single case. Any U.S. commercial dispute or international arbitration with strong merits, at any stage; funds businesses only, not individuals or consumers.
